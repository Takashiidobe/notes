# Creamy Garlic Pasta

- From: [link](https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/269500/creamy-garlic-pasta)

- Cooking Time: 30 minutes

## Ingredients:

- 2 teaspoons olive oil
- 4 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 0.5 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 0.25 teaspoon salt
- 0.5 pound spaghetti
- 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 0.75 cup heavy cream
- 1.5 tablespoons dried parsley

## Instructions:

Gather all ingredients.

Heat olive oil in a medium pan over medium heat. Add garlic and stir until fragrant, 1 to 2 minutes. Add butter and stir constantly until melted.

Boil pasta; add pepper and salt, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 12 minutes.

Add Parmesan cheese, cream, and parsley and mix until thoroughly combined. Serve immediately.
