# SHRIMP SCAMPI

- From: [link](https://www.babi.sh/recipes/shrimp-scampi-basics)

## Ingredients:

- 2 tsp kosher salt (+ more to taste)
- 1/2 lb linguine pasta
- 3 tbsp unsalted butter
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 3 garlic cloves, crushed
- 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
- 1/2 cup dry white wine
- 1 lb frozen shrimp, defrosted
- 1 tsp freshly ground pepper
- 1 large lemon, juiced
- 2 tbsp parsley, chopped
- 1/4 cup reserved pasta water
- Salt & pepper to taste

## Instructions:

For Simple Shrimp Scampi:

Fill a large pot ⅔ full with water, season with salt, and bring to a boil. - 2 tsp kosher salt (+ more to taste)

In a large skillet, melt butter over medium-high heat. - 3 tbsp unsalted butter

Add crushed garlic and red pepper flakes. Cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds. - 3 garlic cloves crushed - ¼ tsp crushed red pepper flakes

Add linguine to the boiling water and cook until al dente according to package instructions. - ½ lb linguine pasta

Add white wine to the skillet and cook until reduced by half, about 3-5 minutes. - ½ cup dry white wine

Add shrimp to the skillet, cooking 45 seconds to 1 minute per side until pink. - 1 lb frozen shrimp defrosted

Add lemon juice, stirring to incorporate into the sauce. - 1 large lemon juiced

Drain pasta, reserving ¼ cup of pasta water. - ½ lb linguine pasta - ¼ cup reserved pasta water

Add pasta to the skillet, tossing to combine. If the sauce looks too thin or separated, add the reserved pasta water. - ½ lb linguine pasta - ¼ cup reserved pasta water

Season with salt and pepper. - Salt & pepper to taste
